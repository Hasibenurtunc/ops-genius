CUSTOMER SUPPORT POLICY

1. CONTACTING CUSTOMER SERVICE
Our customer support team is available Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM. 
Customers can reach us via live chat, email, or phone. Average response 
time is under 2 hours during business hours.

2. CONTACTING A HUMAN AGENT
Customers who prefer to speak with a human agent can request this 
through live chat or by calling our support line. Wait times may vary 
depending on volume. Human agents are available during business hours only.

3. SUBMITTING A COMPLAINT
Customers can submit a complaint through our website, email, or by 
speaking with a support agent. All complaints are acknowledged within 
24 hours and resolved within 5 business days. Customers receive a 
complaint reference number for tracking purposes.

4. SUBMITTING A REVIEW
Customers can submit product and service reviews through their order 
history page. Reviews must comply with our community guidelines. 
Offensive or inappropriate reviews will be removed.

5. NEWSLETTER SUBSCRIPTION
Customers can subscribe or unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time 
through account settings or via the unsubscribe link in any newsletter 
email. We send newsletters maximum twice per week.
