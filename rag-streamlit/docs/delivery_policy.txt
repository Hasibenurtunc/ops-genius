DELIVERY POLICY

1. DELIVERY OPTIONS / SHIPMENT METHODS
Our delivery options, also referred to as shipment methods or shipping
methods, include the following choices for receiving your order:
- Standard Delivery (5-7 business days)
- Express Delivery (2-3 business days)
- Next Day Delivery (order before 2 PM)

Delivery options and costs are displayed at checkout. To see available
shipment methods, visit the checkout page or your account settings.
You can check what shipping options are available during the ordering
process.

2. DELIVERY PERIODS
Standard delivery takes 5-7 business days. Express delivery takes
2-3 business days. Next day delivery is available for orders placed
before 2 PM on business days. Delivery times may vary during peak
seasons and holidays.

3. SETTING UP A SHIPPING ADDRESS
Customers can add multiple shipping addresses to their account.
A default shipping address can be selected in account settings.
P.O. boxes are accepted for standard delivery only.

4. INTERNATIONAL DELIVERY / SHIPPING TO OTHER COUNTRIES
We deliver to over 50 countries worldwide. If you are ordering from
a different city or country, we can ship to your location as long as
it is within our supported delivery regions.

International delivery takes 7-14 business days. Customs duties and
taxes are the responsibility of the customer and vary by country.
To check if we deliver to your city or country, visit the checkout
page and enter your delivery address.

We currently ship to most cities and countries in Europe, North America,
Asia, and Australia. If your delivery city or country is not supported,
you will see a notification at checkout.

5. FAILED DELIVERIES
If a delivery attempt fails, the courier will leave a notification.
Customers have 5 business days to arrange redelivery or collection.
After this period, the item is returned to us and a refund is issued.

We ship to many countries and cities worldwide including Finland, Germany,
France, Spain, Italy, United Kingdom, United States, Canada, and Australia.
To check if we deliver to your specific city or country, please visit the
checkout page and enter your delivery address.

Customers can choose how their order gets to them by selecting a delivery
method at checkout. Available shipping choices include Standard, Express,
and Next Day Delivery.
