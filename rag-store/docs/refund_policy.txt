REFUND AND PAYMENT POLICY

1. REQUESTING A REFUND
Customers are eligible for a full refund within 30 days of purchase. 
To initiate a refund, customers must provide their Order Number and 
reason for return. Refunds are only applicable to items in their 
original condition. Digital and personalized products are non-refundable.

2. REFUND PROCESSING TIME
Once a refund request is approved, the amount is returned to the 
original payment method within 5-7 business days. Customers will 
receive a confirmation email when the refund is processed.

3. TRACKING A REFUND
Customers can track their refund status using their Order Number on 
our website. Refund statuses include: Requested, Approved, Processing, 
and Completed.

4. CANCELLATION FEE POLICY
Orders cancelled within 24 hours of placement are not subject to any 
cancellation fee. Orders cancelled after 24 hours but before shipment 
may incur a 5% cancellation fee. Orders that have already been shipped 
cannot be cancelled and must go through the return process.

5. ACCEPTED PAYMENT METHODS
We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, PayPal, and bank transfers. 
Prepaid cards and cryptocurrency are not accepted. All transactions are 
processed in USD and are encrypted with SSL security.

6. PAYMENT ISSUES
If a payment fails, customers should verify their card details and 
billing address. If the problem persists, customers should contact 
their bank or reach out to our support team with their Order Number.

7. INVOICE REQUESTS
Customers can request an invoice for any completed order within 60 days 
of purchase. Invoices are sent to the registered email address within 
2 business days.
