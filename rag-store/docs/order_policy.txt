ORDER MANAGEMENT POLICY

1. PLACING AN ORDER
Customers can place orders through our website or mobile application 24/7. 
Each order receives a unique Order Number upon confirmation. Customers must 
ensure their shipping address and payment details are correct before 
completing the purchase. Once an order is placed, a confirmation email 
is sent within 5 minutes.

2. CANCELLING AN ORDER
Customers may cancel their order within 24 hours of placement at no charge. 
To cancel, customers must provide their Order Number. If the order has 
already been shipped, cancellation is no longer possible and the customer 
must follow the return process instead. Orders that contain personalized 
or digital items cannot be cancelled once placed.

3. CHANGING AN ORDER
Order modifications (such as quantity or item changes) are only allowed 
within 12 hours of placement. After this window, changes cannot be 
guaranteed. Customers must contact support with their Order Number to 
request a modification.

4. TRACKING AN ORDER
Customers can track their order status using their Order Number on our 
website or via the tracking link sent by email. Order statuses include: 
Processing, Shipped, Out for Delivery, and Delivered. Tracking information 
is updated every 24 hours.

5. CHANGING SHIPPING ADDRESS
A shipping address can only be changed if the order has not yet been 
shipped. Customers must contact support immediately with their Order Number 
and the new address. Once the order is in transit, address changes are 
not possible.

Customers who can no longer afford an order or change their mind after
purchasing may cancel their order within 24 hours of placement at no charge.
The word "purchase" refers to any order placed on our platform.

Customers who wish to undo, reverse, or stop a recent purchase or buy
may cancel their order within 24 hours of placement. Stopping a shipment
before it leaves the warehouse is possible only if the order has not yet
been shipped. Contact support immediately with your order details.
