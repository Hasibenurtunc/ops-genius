ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT POLICY

1. CREATING AN ACCOUNT
Customers can create a free account using a valid email address and
password. Account registration requires name, email, and password.
A verification email is sent upon registration and must be confirmed
within 48 hours. If the verification email is not received, customers
can request a new one from the login page. Accounts created with
social login (Google, Facebook) do not require a separate password.

2. EDITING ACCOUNT INFORMATION
Customers can update their name, email, phone number, and password
at any time through account settings. Changes to email address require
re-verification. Payment information can be updated in the billing section.
Profile changes are saved immediately and take effect right away.

3. RECOVERING A PASSWORD / PIN / ACCESS KEY
Your account password may also be referred to as a PIN code, access key,
user key, profile PIN, or login credentials. All of these terms refer to
the same thing: the secret code you use to log in to your account.
If you have forgotten, lost, or need to reset your password, PIN code,
access key, or any login credential, follow the steps below.

To reset your password, PIN, or access key:
- Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password" or "Reset PIN"
- Enter the registered email address
- Check the inbox for a password reset email
- Click the reset link within 30 minutes
- Enter and confirm the new password or PIN
- Log in with the new credentials

If you cannot remember your PIN code or access key:
- Use the "Forgot Password" option on the login page
- A reset link will be sent to your registered email address
- Follow the instructions in the email to create a new PIN or password

If you have lost access to your account key or user credentials:
- Contact customer support with proof of identity
- The support team will verify your identity and help you restore access

Customers who forget their password can use the "Forgot Password" option
on the login page. A password reset link is sent to the registered email
address and expires after 30 minutes.

If a customer cannot access their registered email address, they should
contact customer support with proof of identity to recover their account.

Steps to reset a password:
- Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password"
- Enter the registered email address
- Check the inbox for a password reset email
- Click the reset link within 30 minutes
- Enter and confirm the new password
- Log in with the new password

Common password recovery issues:
- Reset email not received: Check spam or junk folder. If still not
  found, request a new reset link. Ensure the correct email address
  is being used.
- Reset link expired: Request a new password reset link from the
  login page. Links expire after 30 minutes for security reasons.
- Account locked after multiple failed attempts: Wait 15 minutes
  before trying again, or contact support to unlock the account.
- Forgot registered email address: Contact customer support with
  your full name and order history to verify identity and recover
  the account.

Passwords must be at least 8 characters long and include a combination
of letters and numbers. Customers are advised to use a unique password
and not share it with anyone.

4. ACCOUNT ACCESS ISSUES
Customers who cannot log in should first verify that the correct email
address and password are being used. If login fails repeatedly, the
account may be temporarily locked for security purposes.

To unlock a locked account:
- Wait 15 minutes and try again
- Use the "Forgot Password" option to reset credentials
- Contact customer support if the issue persists

Customers who believe their account has been compromised or accessed
by an unauthorized person should immediately reset their password and
contact support. The support team can review recent account activity
and help secure the account.

5. REGISTRATION PROBLEMS
If customers experience issues during registration, they should ensure
their email address is not already linked to an existing account.
Common registration errors include invalid email format, weak password,
or a duplicate account. For persistent issues, customers should contact
support with a description of the error and a screenshot if possible.

6. SWITCHING ACCOUNTS
Customers managing multiple accounts must log out before switching.
Merging of accounts is not supported. Each account must have a unique
email address. Customers can log out from the account menu at the
top right corner of the page.

7. DELETING AN ACCOUNT
Customers may request permanent account deletion at any time. Upon
deletion, all personal data is removed within 30 days in accordance
with GDPR regulations. Active orders must be completed or cancelled
before account deletion. Deleted accounts cannot be recovered.
To delete an account, go to Account Settings and select Delete Account,
or contact customer support.
